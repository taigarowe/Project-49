Interesting Facts About Camel

  • Camels are known as "the ship of desert" because they can walk easily on sand.
  • They can store water , and survive without drinking water for several days.

Interesting Facts About Chameleon

  • This reptile can changes its color for camouflaging.
  • They can see a much greater view as comparitive to humans.

Interesting Facts About Flying Cicada

  • They have the usual insect modes of locomotion, walking and flight.
  • they do not walk or run well, and take to the wing to travel distances greater than a few centimetres.

Interesting Facts About Panda

  • They sleep for 2-3 hours at a time, and then spend 3-4 hours eating.
  • This sleeping mode can sustain a stable energy intake, and explains why they sleep right after they are full and eat right after they are awake, just like they do in a zoo.

Interesting Facts About Pigeon

  • Pigeons are incredibly complex and intelligent animal
  • They can see 360 degrees
